# Pediatric Neurology Educational Materials

Welcome to the KUMC Pediatric Neurology lecture repository. This site provides comprehensive educational materials for medical students and residents.

---

## Available Lectures

### [Pediatric Neurology: A Comprehensive Guide](Pediatric_Neurology_Lecture.md)

A complete lecture covering seizures, epilepsy, and modern approaches to diagnosis and treatment.

**Topics Include:**
- Seizure definitions and semiology
- ILAE 2017/2022/2025 classification system
- Age-dependent EEG interpretation
- Genetic epilepsies and major genes
- First seizure evaluation algorithm
- Precision medicine approaches
- Clinical pearls and emergency considerations

## Quick Reference Tables

### Major Epilepsy Genes

| Gene | Channel/Function | Key Phenotype | Treatment Note |
|------|------------------|---------------|----------------|
| SCN1A | Nav1.1 | Dravet syndrome | **AVOID** Na+ blockers |
| KCNQ2 | Kv7.2 | Neonatal DEE | Na+ blockers effective |
| STXBP1 | Synaptic release | Early DEE | LEV may help |
| CDKL5 | Kinase | CDKL5-DEE | Limited ASM efficacy |

### EEG: Posterior Dominant Rhythm by Age

| Age | PDR Frequency |
|-----|---------------|
| 6 months | 4-5 Hz |
| 1 year | 5-6 Hz |
| 3 years | 7-8 Hz |
| 10 years | 9-10 Hz |
